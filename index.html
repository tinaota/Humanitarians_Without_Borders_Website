Who We Are

A Community Organisation Built for Communities

Established in 2017, Humanitarians Without Borders is a Victorian-based incorporated multicultural community organisation supporting migrant, refugee and culturally diverse communities in the western suburbs of Melbourne and surrounding regions.

Driven by 6 core volunteers and an extended volunteer network, we work alongside grassroots groups, faith communities and local leaders to strengthen organisational capacity, improve governance, and deliver programs that promote safety, wellbeing and social inclusion.

We are an incorporated association registered with Consumer Affairs Victoria (Registration No. A0096166U), operating under our own rules with a current and active status.

Our Mission

To empower multicultural communities with the tools, partnerships and support they need to build sustainable futures.

Our Vision

Connected communities where every family feels supported, valued and equipped to succeed.

Inclusion Dignity Collaboration Integrity Community Leadership Accountability
